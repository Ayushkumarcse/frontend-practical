Font Properties Demonstrated

  • font-family: Different font families (serif, sans-serif, monospace, cursive, fantasy)
  • font-size: Various sizes from 1.2em to 3em
  • font-weight: Normal and bold
  • font-style: Normal and italic
  • text-shadow: Adds depth to text

Background Image Properties Demonstrated

  • background-image: Sets the image source
  • background-repeat: repeat, no-repeat
  • background-size: cover, contain, or specific dimensions
  • background-position: center, top, bottom, etc.
  • background-attachment: fixed or scroll
