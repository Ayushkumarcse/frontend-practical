CSS Box Model: Margin, Padding, Border, and Content

Content Box
Margin (30px) → Border (5px) → Padding (20px) → Content

Explanation:

  • Margin: 30px (space outside the border)
  • Border: 5px solid green (border around padding)
  • Padding: 20px (space inside border, around content)
  • Content: The actual content area
